Services

What I Do

I am currently only offering trumpet teaching & consultation, but this may be subject to change in the future!

01
Trumpet Teaching & Consultation

Playing instruments since a young age, and playing the trumpet for many years, I have decided to spread the joy I get from learning the trumpet. Currently, sessions are only available in the Greater Toronto Area for $20/hour. All sessions are done in person to maximize student success.

02
Marching Arts

I have been active in the marching arts for 2 years, primarily playing the trumpet. I have also been a drill writer/freestyle planner and have been contributing to the freestyle planning team. I am currently not offering any marching arts services, but this may change in the future!

03
Composing

Composing since 2023, I have been writing music for a variety of reasons. I am currently not offering any composing services, but this may change in the future!

04
Conducting

Learning to conduct with Ontario's finest musicians and conductors, I have been glad to enjoy the art of conducting. I am currently not offering any conducting services, but this may change in the future!
